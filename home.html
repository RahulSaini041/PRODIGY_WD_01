Welcome to the Golden Era

Our Story

Stephen and Juliette Wall opened The Golden Era after they travelled to Vietnam and fell in love with the food.

In 2005, they made the decision to start a restaurant serving phở while sat on high stools, slurping pho bo, in Ho Chi Minh City.

All these years later, the golden era is a group of restaurants dotted around the country, serving great value, healthy Vietnamese food that's cooked fresh in each kitchen daily.
